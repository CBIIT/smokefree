what is #OnKilter?

It’s a social media campaign for teens, fueled by health challenges. The Smokefree.gov initiative, in collaboration with partners, will issue weekly challenges that ask teens to do something healthy. They respond with visual evidence (photo, video) of themselves completing the challenge. Each week, we’ll repost some of the best submissions and show how teens around the country are stepping up to the challenge to be healthier.

STEP 1
Check our Instagram on FRIDAYS for challenges
STEP 2
Take a pic or video of yourself doing the challenge.
STEP 3
Post it to Instagram and tag it with #OnKilter
We will choose posts to feature on Instagram each Thursday.
